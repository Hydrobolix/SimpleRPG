Player Info

HP/MP - Your health/mana

ATK/DEF - Physical attack/def

MATK/MDEF - Magic attack/defense

SPD - Speed, influences turn order and number of normal attacks

Tip: If you are at least 2x faster than the enemy, you gain an additional normal attack (plan to increase that later)

Reincarnation

Allows you to reincarnate your character, giving you the option to change to another base class.
You keep 10% of your current stats as part of the reincarnation bonus, except HP and MP.

Tip: Reincarnate in the beginnging to get a small boost.

Skill Types

Active - Generally damage skills that directly hit the enemy.

Passive - Increase your stats

Temp - Temporary buffs/debuffs that last until the battle ends.

Summon - An ally that join you in battle. Each turn uses a bit of their MP to use an action. When they run out of MP, they heal you using a portion of their HP.
A summon can only be used once per battle.

Transform - Allows you to transform, changing your stats. (Plans: Additional skills for some transformations)

Status Afflictions

Poison - Deals a bit of damage each turn. Eventually wears off.

Confusion - Chance for the enemy to attack themselves. Eventually wears off.

Stun - Prevents the enemy from doing their next action.

A few more in the game, with plans for more in the future.
